03

Certified Inspection

Our 120-point inspection is the backbone of the Bro Cars promise. Led by certified mechanical engineers, every vehicle is evaluated across four categories: structural integrity, mechanical condition, electrical systems, and interior/exterior cosmetics. All findings are fully documented and shared with buyers. We don't hide problems — we fix them or price transparently. If a vehicle fails our standards, it never reaches our forecourt. We also offer standalone inspection services for third-party vehicles.

  • ✓ 120-point engineering check
  • ✓ Full written report provided
  • ✓ Third-party inspection service
  • ✓ 6-month powertrain warranty

Trade-In & Leasing

Upgrading your current vehicle? Our trade-in programme delivers a fast, fair market valuation within 24 hours. We handle all documentation, transfer fees, and administrative work — so you can focus on your next dream drive. For clients who want maximum return, our consignment service lists your vehicle in our premium showroom with full marketing support. We charge a transparent commission on successful sale only — no upfront fees. Leasing options available for corporate and personal clients.

  • ✓ 24-hour vehicle valuation
  • ✓ Full admin & transfer handled
  • ✓ Consignment with no upfront fees
