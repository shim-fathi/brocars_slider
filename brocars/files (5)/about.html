Who We Are

More Than a Dealership

Bro Cars was founded in 2012 by Arjun Perera, a car enthusiast with a simple but powerful idea: that buying a vehicle should be an experience to celebrate, not a process to dread. Starting with just six vehicles in a modest Colombo showroom, Arjun built the business on three uncompromising principles — honesty, quality, and genuine customer care.

Over the past 12 years, we've grown into one of Sri Lanka's most recognized automotive brands, with a showroom spanning over 8,000 sq ft, a team of 25 dedicated professionals, and an inventory that consistently turns over more than 500 vehicles annually. Yet despite our growth, the culture remains deeply personal. Every customer who walks through our doors is treated like family.

We specialize in premium European and Japanese vehicles, carefully sourced from verified private sellers, auction houses, and authorized importers. Each car is photographed, inspected, and priced transparently — because we believe you deserve the full picture before making one of the biggest financial decisions of your life.

🎯 Our Mission

To make premium vehicle ownership accessible and transparent, delivering exceptional value and trust at every step of the journey.

🔭 Our Vision

To become South Asia's most trusted automotive dealership, recognized for integrity, innovation, and outstanding after-sales support.
