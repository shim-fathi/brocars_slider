Premium Vehicle Sales

Our core service: connecting you with the right vehicle at the right price. We maintain a rotating inventory of 60–80 premium vehicles at any time, spanning luxury sedans, performance SUVs, electric cars, and family hatchbacks. Every listing includes a full transparent spec sheet, documented service history, and an honest assessment of condition. Our sales advisors are non-commissioned, meaning their only goal is to find you the perfect fit — not the highest margin. We also offer bespoke sourcing: tell us what you want, and we'll find it within 14 days or less.

Flexible Financing

Owning your dream car shouldn't mean draining your savings. Bro Cars partners with seven leading Sri Lankan banks and leasing companies to offer competitive financing tailored to your situation. Whether you're a first-time buyer, a business owner, or upgrading from a current vehicle, our finance team will structure a package that works — with clear terms, no hidden charges, and fast approvals. We also offer balloon payment plans and step-up EMI options for those who prefer flexibility in repayment. Most approvals are confirmed within 48 hours.

Certified Pre-Sale Inspection

Our 120-point inspection process is the cornerstone of the Bro Cars promise. Conducted by our in-house certified mechanical engineers, every vehicle is evaluated across four key categories: structural integrity, mechanical condition, electrical systems, and interior/exterior cosmetics. Findings are documented in a detailed report shared with every buyer. We don't hide problems — we fix them or price accordingly. If a vehicle fails our standards, it never reaches our forecourt. We also offer standalone inspection services for vehicles you're considering purchasing elsewhere, giving you independent, expert peace of mind.

🔄
Trade-In & Consignment

Upgrading your current vehicle? Let us take the hassle out of selling. Our trade-in programme offers a fast, fair market valuation on your existing car — usually delivered within 24 hours. We handle all documentation, transfer fees, and admin, so you can focus on choosing your next dream drive. For clients who prefer maximum return, our consignment service lists your vehicle in our premium showroom and online channels, with our sales team actively marketing it on your behalf. We charge a transparent commission only on successful sale — no upfront fees, no hidden deductions.
